About Me

Creative

Avid Learner

I am a Fullstack Web Developer based out of Albuquerque New Mexico with a passion for learning and creating visually enticing projects.
I studied Fine Arts for two years at Central New Mexico Community College and over two years at the University of New Mexico.
I take pride in my work and want to be a positive influence on my community. I strive to be a resource for Hispanic women,
offering my experience and guidance on how to overcome some of the challenges we face. I would love to connect with
like-minded people and organizations in the industry willing to partake in helping the tech industry to become more diverse and inclusive.
I’m grateful and proud of my rich cultural heritage and want to offer my perspective and insight to encourage outreach and education
for Hispanic women interested in tech. I strongly believe that diversity would benefit and push the industry into a new era.
